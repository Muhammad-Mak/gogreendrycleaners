# Eco-Friendly Dry Cleaning Myths: Separating Fact from Fiction

May 6, 2026

![Image 2: Go-Green | Eco-Friendly Dry Cleaning Myths: Separating Fact from Fiction](https://gogreendrycleaners.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/front-view-disappointed-young-couple-talking-each-other-while-washing-clothes-white-wall-1.jpg)

Eco-friendly dry cleaning has grown in popularity, but misinformation still surrounds it. Let’s debunk some common myths and uncover the truth about going green with your garment care.

### **Myth #1: Eco-Friendly Cleaning Isn’t as Effective**

**Fact:** Non-toxic solvents clean just as deeply as traditional ones. Wet cleaning and silicone-based methods can remove tough stains like oil, makeup, and wine without damaging fabrics.

### **Myth #2: It’s More Expensive**

**Fact:** While prices are comparable, green cleaning helps clothes last longer — saving you money over time by reducing wear and tear.

### **Myth #3: It’s Only for Delicates**

**Fact:** Eco-friendly cleaning works on everything — from tailored suits to outerwear, wedding gowns, and linens.

### **Myth #4: It Takes Longer**

**Fact:** With modern equipment, eco-friendly cleaning delivers quick turnarounds — often within the same timeframe as traditional services.

### **Myth #5: “Green” Means the Same Everywhere**

**Fact:** Many cleaners claim to be eco-friendly without meeting true sustainability standards. (See our article on _greenwashing_ for what to look for.)
