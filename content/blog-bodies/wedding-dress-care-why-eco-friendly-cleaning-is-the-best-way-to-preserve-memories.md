### **The Risks of Traditional Cleaning**

*   **Harsh chemicals** can yellow or damage fragile fabrics.
*   **Residue from perc solvents** may linger, causing discoloration over time.
*   **Improper storage** can trap moisture and lead to stains or mildew.

*   **Detailed pre-inspection** to identify stains, tears, and fabric type.

*   **Fabric-specific treatments** for lace, silk, chiffon, and satin.
