At Go Green Dry Cleaners, sustainability isn’t just a slogan — it’s our standard. From the cleaning methods we use to the way we package each garment, every part of our process is designed to care for your clothes _and_ the environment.

Across all our locations throughout South Florida, New York and Connecticut, our team is redefining what it means to be a truly eco-friendly cleaner. Here’s a look behind the scenes at how we do it.

## **Step 1: A Thoughtful Inspection Process**

Before any garment is cleaned, our experts carefully inspect it for stains, wear, and fabric type. This step ensures each item receives customized care — because a silk blouse and a wool suit shouldn’t be treated the same way.

*   **Detailed Tagging System:** Tracks every garment throughout the process.
*   **Stain Mapping:** Identifies problem areas before cleaning.
*   **Fabric Evaluation:** Ensures the right cleaning solution and temperature are used.

Every detail matters — that’s why we treat each piece as if it were our own.

## **Step 2: Non-Toxic, Eco-Friendly Cleaning Methods**

Unlike traditional dry cleaners that use perchloroethylene (perc), a chemical solvent known to be harsh on fabrics and harmful to the environment, Go Green uses **non-toxic, biodegradable cleaning methods** that are safe for your clothes, your home, and your planet.

### **Our Eco-Cleaning Technology Includes:**

*   **Wet Cleaning:** A water-based process that uses biodegradable detergents and controlled temperatures to gently clean delicate fabrics.
*   **Silicone-Based Solvents:** Derived from sand, these solvents are gentle, odor-free, and safe for all fabric types.
*   **Energy-Efficient Machines:** Reduce water and electricity consumption, minimizing our carbon footprint.

The result? **Garments that look better, feel softer, and last longer — without exposing your skin or the environment to harmful chemicals.**

## **Step 3: Expert Finishing & Fabric Care**

After cleaning, garments go through precise finishing and pressing using eco-friendly steam equipment.

*   **No harsh heat or chemicals** that damage fibers.
*   **Gentle steaming** restores natural drape and texture.
*   **Professional pressing** ensures crisp lines and a polished finish.

Whether it’s a business suit in Stamford or a sundress in Boca Raton, your clothes are returned in pristine, ready-to-wear condition.

## **Step 4: Sustainable Packaging Practices**

We know sustainability extends beyond cleaning — it includes everything from hangers to garment bags.

Here’s how we reduce waste every day:

*   **Hanger Recycling:** Customers can return hangers to be sanitized and reused.
*   **Biodegradable Garment Covers:** Replaces single-use plastic.
*   **Reusable Pickup Bags:** Available at all locations to minimize packaging waste.

These small steps create a big difference over time — reducing landfill waste and setting a new standard for eco-conscious garment care.

## **Step 5: Serving Our Communities Responsibly**

Go Green isn’t just about cleaner clothes; it’s about **building cleaner communities.**

*   In **South Florida**, our eco-friendly system helps protect beaches, waterways, and marine life by preventing chemical runoff.
*   In **Connecticut and New York**, we reduce emissions and waste in densely populated areas — creating healthier neighborhoods.

Every garment cleaned with us contributes to a cleaner, greener planet.

## **Step 6: Transparency You Can Trust**

We believe customers have the right to know exactly how their clothes are cared for. That’s why we’re fully transparent about our process, from solvent types to recycling efforts. When you choose Go Green, you’re choosing a cleaner that values honesty and sustainability at every step.

## **A Better Way to Care for Clothes**

Go Green Dry Cleaners combines professional expertise with environmental responsibility. Our mission is simple: deliver premium garment care that protects your clothes — and the world around you.
